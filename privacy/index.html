Privacy Policy

Stemacle is built to keep your music on your device. This page explains what the app stores, what it does not collect, and how sharing works when you choose it.

What stays local

Tracks imported into Stemacle stay on the device unless you explicitly export or share them. Project history, queue state, and settings are stored locally so you can reopen your work without signing in.

What we do not collect

  • No account or profile data.
  • No advertising identifiers.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No sale or sharing of your library metadata.

Optional separation server

By default, separation runs entirely on your device and nothing leaves it. Stemacle also offers an optional, higher-quality mode that you turn on by entering a separation server address in Settings. When that mode is on, the audio you choose to split is sent to the server you configured, processed there, and the resulting stems are returned. If no server is configured, no audio is ever uploaded. You control whether this mode is on.

Sharing and exports

If you tap a share or export action, Stemacle passes only the items you chose to the system share sheet or export destination. Those actions are intentional and happen on your command.

Changes

This policy may evolve as Stemacle adds features. The public version will stay linked from the app so App Store review and users can always find it.
