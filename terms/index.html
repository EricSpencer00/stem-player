What Stemacle does

Stemacle separates audio you provide into drums, bass, vocals, and melody stems, and lets you play, loop, and export them. Separation runs on your device by default. You may optionally point the app at a separation server you control for higher-quality results; in that case the audio you choose to split is sent to that server to be processed and returned.
